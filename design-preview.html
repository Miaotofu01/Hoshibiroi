The Prepared Mind

The history of science is full of serendipity — penicillin, the microwave oven, even the humble sticky note were all stumbled upon by accident.

What turns a lucky accident into a real breakthrough is a prepared mind: someone ready to notice the moment when something unexpected quietly turns out to matter.
